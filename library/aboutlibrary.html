VPIMSR LIBRARY

Mission: A library is a curated collection of sources of information and similar resources, selected by experts and made accessible to a defined community for reference or borrowing. It provides physical or digital access to material, and may be a physical location or a virtual space, or both. Library is place which reading materials, such as books, periodicals, and newspapers, and often other materials such as musical and video recording, are kept for use or lending.


Goals:

  • VPIMSR Library is dedicated to providing quality service and an admirable collection of materials to support the mission, goals, educational, and research needs of students, staff, faculty, administration, and community users and in support of the Institution's various curriculum.

  • The mission of the VP Library is to achieve excellence in the provision and promotion of information services to meet the teaching and learning needs.

Library committee: The Institute has a standing Library Committee authorized by the Management. The committee inclusive of 06 faculty members and librarian as a member secretary. The committee meets twice a year and reviews all the activities of the library & recommends to take further improvements. Important decisions are noted down and action taken to implement the same. Monitoring of the use of library is being done by the committee regularly.

Librarians involvement in the academic activities: Librarian is the member of staff council & various sub committees and also involved directly & indirectly in the academic activities of the institute. Besides being involved in the activities of the institute, librarian is involved in many other academic activities outside the college, viz, teaching B.Lib.Sc.and M.Lib. Students of YCMOU University, Nasik. and also interested in cultural activities like Drama, program announcers etc,

Book selection & purchase: New books are recommended & selected for purchase by the Heads of respective departments & the library purchases through regular procedure & they are exhibited in the library after their receipt and the respective Heads of Department are informed on receipt regularly.
